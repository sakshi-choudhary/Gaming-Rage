Type Master

A game where user can type the given text in a limited amount of time and at last can check their no. of errors and accuracy %.

Instructions!

  • See the time interval words must be typed in displayed in the app window
  • Click on the 'Stop Practice' button to stop the practice session
  • You will be prompted if an incorrect letter is entered
  • You will be able to Resume after you stop
  • Click on the 'play again' button during a game or after it finishes
  • You can check their errors and accuracy % at the end
  • You can see correct letter he typed in green and incorrect in red during the game
